Welcome to IEC Group Nepal

Education...Our Passion

IEC Group established in the year 1997 under the visionary leadership of Ms. Shailaja Adhikary, Managing Director (IEC Group) has emerged as the pioneer in Education sector of Nepal & number one educational institution of Fashion & Interior Design in Nepal with an unprecedented growth rate of more than 500%. IEC Group is one of the fastest growing education groups in Nepal.

IEC Group Nepal

Profile

IEC Group runs 12 prominent educational institutions in Nepal

  • Four Pre Schools (Euro kids at Tangal, Bansbari, Samakhushi & Tinkune) in Kathmandu, Nepal, the largest pre school chain of Nepal spread in over 70000 sq.ft)
  • One Main Stream school (Euro School spread in over 45000 sq.ft) at Hadigaun, Kathmandu, Nepal
  • Two Colleges for Fashion & Interior (IEC College of Art & Fashion) spread in over 30000 sq.ft area at Bishalnagar & Chandol, Kathmandu, Nepal
  • Two Professional institution of Fashion & Interior (IEC School of Art & Fashion spread in over 6750sq.ft) at Bagbazar, Kathmandu & Sallaghari, Bhaktapur
  • Manya Abroad Studies (The Princeton Review, world leader in Test Prep (GRE, GMAT, SAT, IELTS, TOEFL and Overseas Education) spread in over 3300 sq.ft at Bhatbhateni, Kathmandu, Nepal
  • ETTI (Nepal) (Teacher Training Centre) spread in over 2000 sq.ft at Bhatbhateni, Kathmandu, Nepal
  • Lets Talk (Learning Centre for English Communications Skill) spread in over 2000 sq.ft at Bagbazar, Kathmandu, Nepal
  • Promoter & Organizer of Nepal Fashion Week since its inception in 2004
  • Under pipeline (BBA & MBA College) in Maharjgunj, Kathmandu and 3 more Pre-Schools in Kathmandu

Ms. Shailaja Adhikary

Shailaja Adhikary, an MBA in marketing, is a well known figure in both the Education and Fashion fields. Serving as the Managing Director of nine prominent educational institutes in Nepal, she was rightly chosen as the top 12 entrepreneurs of Nepal and the only lady among them by Kantipur Publications in May 2012 & recently as Best Women Entrepreneur at New Business Age Award 2013 held at Hotel Soaltee Crown Plaza on 24th Aug 2013.


She’s a visionary who changed the face of Nepal by introducing an International pre-school chain i.e. Euro Kids and Fashion & Interior Design Education in Nepal. One of the many feathers in her cap is having the highest press coverage for any educational institute in Nepal, IEC School of Art & Fashion, of which she is the Managing Director.


She was chosen as GREAT WOMAN OF THE 21STCENTURY in 2006 by AMERICAN BIOGRAPHICAL INSTITUTE, North Carolina, USA & the Educational Excellence Award 2012 held at IIT, Delhi (India).


She is truly the pride of Nepal. Her drive to excel and to contribute to her society continues as she is opening an MBA college, Consultancy & Teacher’s Training Institutes shortly in Kathmandu.
