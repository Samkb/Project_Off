Releases

These are the released albums of KUTUMBA

ALBUMS

2004 KUTUMBA: EVERGREEN NEPALI FOLK TUNES

Kutumba album was realeased on date 2004, the album was entirely a collection of Nepali evergreen Folk Tunes. The songs in the album soon got into the ears and hand of the people. It is still one of the best album sales in the instrumental section of Nepali Music.

2005 FOLK ROOTS

After the immence success of 1st album the band released the second album in 2005, this album is too band’s effort to revive the evergreen Nepali Folk Tunes in the nepali music scene.

2007 NAULO BIHANI

Released on 2007, this album is a mix of both evergreen Nepali Folk Tunes as well as few of Kutumba’s original too. The originals from the album was accepted by the general public and was played in most of the media houses.

2009 MITHILA

Released on 2009, this album was the dedication to the Maithili Music (a musically rich community from the plains of Nepal). The band a intensive research on the music there and also as a contribution the album was released.

2012 USARGA

Released in 2012,This album is entirely band originals. All the songs we based on the Nepali folk roots as well as the influence of different genre of music.

2013 KARMATH

Realeased 2014, This is album is also band original. This album is the outcome of the collaborative effort of the band, experimentation through different genre music and the artists around the globe.

2017HIMALAYAN HIGHLANDS

Realeased 2017
Himalayan Highlands is the outcome of the band’s journey to the Highlands of the Scotland. The cross cultural inbound of the folk music from different country. The geographic distribution of the Highlands of Scotland and the hills of Nepal were very similar.
