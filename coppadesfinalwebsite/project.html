Project


   
   

Construction of Housing

The 6,000 square foot dormitory is complete! This will sleep eighty-eight students. This two-story dormitory will house students preparing for ministry. 

At this time there are plans for a larger dormitory, two duplexes for teaching staff, completion of the Victoria Chapel and expansion of the cafeteria and Bruton Chapel. These construction projects will be done according to the Master Plan of the college. 

Internet Tower

Through our friends and partners the internet tower was erected in November of 2011! The Arusha Bible College now has internet service. The monthly cost is $75.00 per month. This is for basic service to one computer. We have yet to distribute to the Library, Computer Lab and administrative offices. 

The estimated cost for the equipment and distribution is $3000.00.

Electronic Library

Once the internet is made available to the college, the next step is to create an electronic library. This library would contain such items as Biblical Commentaries, Study Bibles, Reference materials, Biographies, Autobiographies, Topical Studies, etc.

The cost would include the research and the recommendation of materials, the conversion of materials into an electronic format, the transferring of materials to the “server”, and the maintenance of the electronic library.

Ebrary.com is the company being considered for this project.
