Encourage Biomedical Engineering in Nepal

Author:Bhawi Pd. Gurung
May 14, 2017

Biomedical Engineering Association of Nepal (BEAN) was established in 2061 B.S, under the advisory group of Prof. Sam Topham USA and Prof. Uphendra Devkota to established Bio medical engineering in Nepal that is directly related to improve the healthcare services delivery effectively for the people of our country. National conference was held at hotel Del La Annapurna on April 27th 2004 inaugurated by Dr. Nirakar Man Shrestha, Health secretary, Government of Nepal. The theme of the conference was "Need of Biomedical Engineering in Nepal". Altogether 150 personalities were participated including Hospital director's from Kathmandu valley and International Organization GTZ as well NSI representatives at that program.
